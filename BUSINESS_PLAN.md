# Bill Bot Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Bill Bot automates medical bill negotiation for the 100 million Americans carrying medical debt. We fight when patients can't, charging only 20% of savings achieved.

## Market Opportunity

- **$140B** in medical debt held by Americans
- **66.5%** of bankruptcies tied to medical issues
- **Average bill**: $14,000 (hospitals expect ~$3,000)
- **Our opportunity**: Help patients pay what insurers pay

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Manual MVP (Months 1-3)
1. **Recruit 3 negotiators** from hospital billing departments
   - Target: Recently left, know the insider game
   - Compensation: $60K base + 5% of savings
   - Location: Remote (target different regions)

2. **First 100 Customers**
   - Source from medical debt Reddit threads
   - Facebook groups: "Medical Bills Help"
   - Partner with 2-3 patient advocates

3. **Data Collection Focus**
   - Log every call, outcome, script used
   - Track by: Hospital system, charge type, negotiator
   - Build the playbook from real outcomes

### Phase 2: Semi-Automation (Months 4-9)
1. **Technology Build**
   - OCR pipeline for bill extraction
   - CMS pricing database integration
   - Negotiation CRM system
   - Customer portal with real-time updates

2. **Standardize Playbooks**
   - Top 10 overcharges → proven scripts
   - Decision trees for escalation
   - Regulatory leverage points by state

3. **Scale to 10 Negotiators**
   - Hire from customer success backgrounds
   - 2-week training on medical billing
   - Shadow experienced negotiators

### Phase 3: Platform Launch (Months 10-12)
1. **Self-Service Portal**
   - Upload → Analysis → Negotiation start
   - Automated updates via SMS/email
   - Success stories and savings tracker

2. **B2B2C Partnerships**
   - Employee benefit platforms
   - Insurance brokers
   - Healthcare sharing ministries
   - Employers with self-funded plans

## Risk Mitigation

1. **Regulatory Risk**
   - Operate as patient advocate, not law firm
   - Partner with healthcare attorneys
   - State-by-state compliance review

2. **Hospital Pushback**
   - Focus on win-win messaging
   - Faster payment vs. collections
   - Maintain professional relationships

3. **Competition**
   - Build data moat quickly
   - Patent negotiation algorithms
   - Exclusive partnerships with advocates
